## 結論判讀

### Test 1 → score 閘門

- 最強: `f_score` @ fwd_20d: IR=+0.106 (B)
- 有 IR → top picks 顯著優於 bottom picks，可考慮 tighter threshold 或 qm_score 層級過濾

### Test 2 → rank 階梯

- fwd_20d mean by band: 1-5=+2.05%, 6-10=+1.94%, 11-25=+1.48%, 26-50=+0.99%
- **單調遞減**：rank 有區分 → top 10 或 top 20 的收斂有價值，picks 上限可考慮下修

### Test 3 → Scenario 分類

- fwd_20d by scenario: A=+1.22% (n=1230), B=+1.58% (n=1156), C_low=+1.21% (n=695), C_mid=+1.47% (n=1842)
- 分類反直覺 (A=+1.22%, B=+1.58%, C_avg=+1.34%) → trend_score 閘門 logic 可能要調

### Test 4 → Entry range fill

- narrow (0.99/1.01) fill rate: 97%
- live (0.98/1.02) fill rate:   99%
- wide (0.95/1.05) fill rate:   100%
- live 區間 fill 組 fwd_20d 比 no-fill 少 7.08% → 區間可能過早進場（沒等更好價）
